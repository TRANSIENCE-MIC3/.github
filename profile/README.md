## TRANSIENCE MIC3

TRANSIENCE is a Horizon Europe project that undertakes a comprehensive characterisation and assessment of circularity principles and measures vis-à-vis decarbonisation, by looking at the twin transition of European industries through the lenses of global competitiveness, innovation, and holistic sustainability.

A key output of TRANSIENCE is MIC3, a fully open-source model framework for assessing industrial circularity, decarbonisation, and sustainability. A series of individual but interoperable modules on the socioeconomic, service and product, material, industrial, energy-system, and environmental perspectives of the transformation of European industry have now been developed, building on and opening the code of leading modelling tools, and will soon be integrated into a fully fledged model ecosystem.

The TRANSIENCE project has received funding from the European Union’s HORIZON EUROPE Research and Innovation Programme under grant agreement No [101137606](https://doi.org/10.3030/101137606).
